Gaston's Hard Boiled Egg Recipe

Description

Eat a dozen eggs to get strong like Gaston!

Ingredients

  • 6-12 Eggs

Steps

  1. Cover the eggs in a saucepan with water about a quarter of the way up the pan (Make sure the water is cold).
  2. Heat the pot on high heat and bring the water to a full rolling boil (Add a teaspoon of vinegar to the water to keep egg whites from running.
  3. Turn off the heat, keep the pan on the hot burner, cover, and let sit for 10-12 mintues.
  4. Strain the water from the pan and run cold water over the eggs to cool them quickly and stop them from cooking further
